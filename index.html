Study Setup & Instructions

Please follow the steps below to install the Chrome extension and complete the study. Keep this tab open while you work.

Extension study Read these instructions before starting

Study Setup

~1–3 min setup
Important: You may need to click the extension icon more than once. Chrome won’t let a webpage automatically open an extension popup.

Setup (one-time)

  1. Install the extension:
    Install Chrome Extension
  2. Open the extension from the toolbar.
    Click the extension icon in Chrome’s toolbar.
    Image placeholder: toolbar icon highlighted (hover/arrow pointing to the extension icon)
  3. Pin the extension to the toolbar.
    Click the puzzle-piece icon → find the study extension → click the pin.
    Image placeholder: “Pin extension” screenshot

Extension workflow

  1. Launch the first website.
    Click the extension icon, then click Launch a website.
  2. Open the panel for the website.
    You’ll be redirected to a website. Because Chrome won’t automatically open the extension popup, click the extension icon again and select Open panel for this page.
  3. Answer all questions (required).
    All questions are mandatory. Use → to go to the next page and ← to go to the previous one.
  4. Review and submit (one submission per site).
    After you answer everything, Review & Submit will automatically be enabled. Click it to review your answers, change if needed, then submit. You can submit only once for a site.
  5. Jump to unanswered questions (if needed).
    Clicking Review Unanswered will take you to the first unanswered question.
  6. Launch the second website.
    After submitting the first site, Launch another website will be enabled. Click it to complete the survey for the next site.
  7. Complete exactly two sites.
    You are required to finish the survey for two sites only.
  8. Finish and remove the extension.
    After you complete the survey, delete/uninstall the extension. If you forget, the extension will be deleted automatically.
